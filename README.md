# Harmony — AI-Powered Couples Coaching

  > **Status: In development. Launching after Eros reaches scale.**
  > Built by the same team behind [SyncWithInsight](https://syncwithinsight.com) — Heather Krueger (CEO) & Tiffany Windsheimer (Co-Founder).

  ---

  ## What is Harmony?

  Harmony is the couples coaching platform that lives inside SyncWithInsight. It's designed for people who *found* their person — and now want to build something lasting with them.

  The same Guardian AI that screened your matches on Eros stays with you inside Harmony — watching for manipulation, celebrating connection, and coaching both of you toward your best relationship.

  ---

  ## Core Features

  | Feature | Description |
  |---------|-------------|
  | **EchoBond** | AI-guided couples sessions that surface what words can't |
  | **Babel Bridge** | Communication translation — what they said vs. what they meant |
  | **Fight Judge** | AI mediates disagreements with zero bias, no sides |
  | **Chores Divider** | Fair labor division with AI equity analysis |
  | **Future Us Simulator** | Visualize your future together with AI-generated life scenarios |
  | **Couples Life Portrait** | A shared personality portrait built from both of your AMBI-181 profiles |
  | **Harmony Shared Space** | A private digital home for you and your partner |
  | **Compliment Jar** | Micro-appreciations that accumulate into relationship capital |
  | **Harmony Pro** | Deep-dive coaching: attachment, love languages, relationship health |
  | **Couples Photos** | AI-generated couple photos for vision-boarding your future |
